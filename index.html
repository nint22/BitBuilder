BitFighter: Build better programs to make more bits!

Description:

This game is educational; it is to show you the power of exponential growth and a bit about computer history / memory sizes. It's also an experiment in UX design: super-simple game, but should have top-notch UI. Should look info-graphic, full-screen vfx

Bits:

A single bit is a binary-digit of information. You want to produce as many of these as fast as possible.

Generators:

Your goal is to build bit-generating entities, so you can build even better generators, all to help you generate even *more* bits!

Buying / Selling:

Click on '+' to buy and '-' to sell a generator. Selling only gives you back 50% of the original cost of purchase.

Generator Price:

The price for each generator grows exponentially the more you buy.
